What is QC?

Checking a product to ensure that it meets:

  • The project goals/expectations
  • The CH standards
  • General best practices

Why is it important?

To ensure quality products

Relates to how many CH values?

  • Put the user’s needs first
  • Make others successful
  • Delight clients with the final product

Types of QC

Split into teams to discuss specific types of QC for your team

  • What does QC look like in your role on a project team?
  • What are the questions you ask when you QC?
  • What tools and systems do you use to QC?
